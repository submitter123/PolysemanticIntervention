Signal in the Noise: Polysemantic Interference Transfers and Predicts Cross-Model Influence

arXiv GitHub

Abstract

We present a novel approach to motion synthesis using diffusion models that achieves state-of-the-art performance across multiple benchmarks. Our method leverages temporal consistency constraints and hierarchical representations to generate realistic and diverse human motions. The proposed framework demonstrates significant improvements over existing methods in terms of both quantitative metrics and qualitative evaluations.
